Your Campus Food Lifeline

Born at Hawassa University, for Hawassa University.

Our Story

Late-night study sessions? Back-to-back classes? That moment you realize you're too busy (or too tired) to leave your dorm for a good meal? We've been there.

E-delivery was born out of our own experiences as students. We saw our friends and classmates at the Main and Techno campuses juggling deadlines, exams, and a social life, with little time left to find a satisfying, affordable meal. We decided to change that.

Our Mission

"To connect you with the best local restaurants and deliver delicious food directly to your campus doorstep, quickly and reliably."

Think of us as your personal food lifeline, bridging the gap between your cravings and your busy schedule. We want you to spend less time worrying about food and more time acing that exam, finishing that project, or just relaxing with friends.

Welcome to hassle-free campus dining. Welcome to E-delivery!
